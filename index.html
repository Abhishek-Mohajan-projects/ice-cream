150 Years of History

In 1870 a young Louis Graeter first started selling ice cream at Cincinnati street markets. It was his widow, Regina, that saw our legacy fully realized into the company you see today. Each generation since has taken the responsibility to uphold the commitment of making the best ice cream, 2 1/2 gallons at a time. Today, five generations later, we proudly remain a family owned and operated company, made possible thanks to our loyal fans.
